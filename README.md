## USA & Canada: The Fine Print That Matters

This is where a lot of people get caught off guard, so let's be clear about it.

Coverage in the US and Canada is limited to 30 days only per year and just for medical emergencies. That limit applies to every policyholder across the board, even if the USA or Canada is your country of citizenship.

Genki Traveler subscribers are limited to 7 days of coverage in the U.S. and Canada, and that coverage only includes emergencies. A "full coverage" add-on eliminates that 7-day limit for an additional monthly fee.

If you're a US citizen planning to visit family back home, this matters. If you're spending most of your time in Southeast Asia or Europe, it probably doesn't affect your day-to-day peace of mind much.
